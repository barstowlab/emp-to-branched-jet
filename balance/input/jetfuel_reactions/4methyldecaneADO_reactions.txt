4-methyl-S-Hydroxyundecanoyl-CoA → 4-methyl-trans-undec-2-enoyl-CoA + H2O

4-methyl-Undecanoyl-CoA + NADH + Hplus → CoA + 4-methyl-Undecanal + NADplus
